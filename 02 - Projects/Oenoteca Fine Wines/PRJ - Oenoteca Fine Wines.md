## Scope of Work

### Phase 1: Housekeeping
- Process 5 draft entries
- Review 4 "In Payment" invoices

### Phase 2: Reconciliation Models
- Set up 11 automated reconciliation models (Fintech, Zelle, Square, Amex, WISE, etc.)

### Phase 3: Bank Reconciliation (BNK1 — 378 lines)
- Internal transfers, recurring expenses, Fintech customer payments
- WISE payments, vendor ACH, Zelle, Square, check deposits

### Phase 4: Bank Reconciliation (BNK2 — 33 lines)
- Check deposits, internal transfers, Chase CC payments

### Phase 5: Missing Vendor Bills
- Create bills for GRW Wine, Joanne USA, Walter Hansel, Enohance Srl, and others

### Phase 6: Chart of Accounts Expansion
- Create missing expense accounts (Rent, Insurance, Vehicle, Telecom, etc.)

### Phase 7: Verification
- Bank balance check, AR/AP verification, suspense account zero-out

### Phase 8: Ongoing Setup
- Reconnect bank sync, establish monthly close process
